BTS (Korean: 방탄소년단; RR: Bangtan Sonyeondan), also known as the Bangtan Boys, is a seven-member South Korean boy band that began formation in 2010 and debuted in 2013 under Big Hit Entertainment.The septet—composed of RM, Jin, Suga, J-Hope, Jimin, V, and Jungkook—co-writes and co-produces much of their own output. Originally a hip hop group, their musical style has evolved to include a wide range of genres. Their lyrics, often focused on personal and social commentary, touch on the themes of mental health, troubles of school-age youth, loss, the journey towards loving oneself, and individualism. Their work features references to literature and psychological concepts and includes an alternative universe storyline. The group has released several albums and performed on several world tours.

With positive messages of self-confidence, intricacies of philosophy hidden in their sparkly songs, true synergy and brotherhood in every step of their elaborate choreography, and countless charitable and anthropological endeavors, BTS have put their 14 best feet forward as role models to millions of adoring fans and anyone else who finds themselves drawn to BTS’s undeniable allure.
They helped people who are suffering from depression by raising their voices in the form of their songs.
Their songs are very inspirational,groovy and catchy and are relatable to youth and other people.

One of the most famous causes BTS has actively been a part of is their anti-violence campaign with UNICEF. Through the BTS Love Myself campaign, the band joins UNICEF Korea to #EndViolence against children and teenagers, with the hope of making the world a better place through their music. Their campaign has not only brought greater awareness of the need to protect young people suffering against violence, but it has also inspired the ARMY to get involved and donate to their cause as well. As of November 2018, the campaign has officially raised $1.4 million in total (1.6 billion KRW). The money donated to the cause came from direct donations of the ARMY, as well as a portion of sales from the band’s ‘Love Yourself’ album series. Donations towards the Love Myself campaign were also out of the members’ own earnings as well.

The band has inspired fans not only through their good deeds, but in their own personal lives as well. Fans not only from the UK, but worldwide, have expressed how much BTS and their music has helped them.

Not just the songs which they produce are amazing but indeed they have amazing personalities.

TIMELINE
12 Jun 2013
BTS officially released their first album "2 Cool for Skool".
13 Jun 2013
BTS had their first Stage Debut performing "No More Dream" at M Countdown and was considered their Debut Date.
11 Jul 2013
BTS announced their Fandom's Official Name which is A.R.M.Y. or Adorable Representative M.C for Youth and is the Birth of ARMYs!
11 Sep 2013
Their First Mini Album "O!RUL8,2?" was released.
13 Nov 2013
BTS wins their first major award when they’re recognised as Best New Artist at South Korea’s Melon Music Awards. They would go on to win many other similar awards throughout the 2013 to 14 awards season.
01 Jan 2014
They won their Second Award "Rookie of the Year" On the 28th Golden Disc Awards.
12 Feb 2014
They won their Third Award "New Artist of the Year" on the 3rd Gaon Chart K-Pop Awards. - After Winning their First award on Gaon Chart K-Pop Awards, Their Second Mini Album "Skool Luv Affair" was released. - On the same day of release, "Boy In Luv" peaks on Billboard World Albums Chart at 3rd Position, Making it their First Biggest Milestone on Billboard World Albums Chart.
Apr-Dec 2014
BTS makes their first trip to the US with their Show & Prove concert in Los Angeles. The boy band released their debut studio album Dark & Wild that features 14 tracks with Danger as its lead single. They performed their first Asian concert outside of Korea by heading to Kobe, Japan as part of their Live Trilogy Episode II: The Red Bullet tour.
23 Jan 2015
BTS won their First award on 25th Seoul Music Awards as the "New Artist of the Year".
Apr-Dec 2015
Their third EP The Most Beautiful Moment In Life, Part 1 debuts on the Heatseekers Albums chart at number six. The group perform on The Show, one of South Korea’s weekly music shows, and win with their song I Need U. A new era is set for the band as their fourth EP The Most Beautiful Moment in Life, Part 2 releases and becomes their first album to debuts on the Billboard 200 at number 171.
02 May 2016
They release their first compilation album The Most Beautiful Moment in Life: Young Forever and it peaks on the Billboard 200 at number 107.
Oct 2016
Their Fourteenth MV "Blood Sweat & Tears" was released, with it's extremely unique Aesthetic Theme which made them More Popular. After the great Impact of Blood Sweat & Tears, Their Second Full Length Album "Wings" was released, One of the most successful and best selling K-Pop album. The band tops the Social 50 Billboard chart for the first time.
21 May 2017
BTS appears at their first Billboard Music Awards where they win the top social artist award, dethroning Justin Bieber who had previous won the award for the last six years.
Oct-Dec 2017
Their song DNA peaks at number 67 on the Hot 100 Billboard, also becoming the highest K-pop song at the time. BTS performs DNA at the AMAs – marking their US TV performance debut. Twitter reveals that BTS is the most tweeted about celebrity for the year. The group continues making history in the US as they become the first K-pop group to perform on the iconic Dick Clark’s New Year’s Rockin’ Eve programme. They perform DNA and Mic Drop.
28 Mar 2018
The Burn the Stage series comes out on YouTube. It follows the 300-day journey of the group’s sold-out Wings Tour.
May 2018
Fake Love gets released as the first single from their new album Love Yourself: Tear. The song debuts at number 10 on the Hot 100, becoming the group’s highest-charting song. BTS releases their third studio album Love Yourself: Tear. It debuts at number one on the Billboard 200 and makes history, as it becomes the first Korean album to top the US charts.
Sep 2018
BTS begins their Love Yourself World tour in Seoul. BTS give a speech at the UN headquarters advising young people to join global efforts against discrimination and poverty. The speech goes viral as #BTSxUnitedNations trends worldwide on Twitter.
2019
They present an award at the 61st Grammy Awards in Los Angeles. The group make history as the first K-pop act to perform as musical guests on Saturday Night Live. They appear in Time 100’s list of most influential people with a paragraph written by American artist Halsey. BTS sells out London’s Wembley Stadium while also making history as the first Korean act to do so.
Feb 2020
BTS performed alongside Lil Nas X, Billy Ray Cyrus, Diplo and Nas at the 62nd Annual Grammy Awards, making BTS the first Korean act to perform at the Grammys. They released their Full-Length Album "Map of The SOUL:7" and its title song "ON" charted at 4th position on Billboard HOT100 Chart.
2020-Present
BTS released their first English-language single, "Dynamite", on August 21. Its music video broke the YouTube record for the most viewed premiere, with more than three million viewers, and set a new record for the most viewed in the first 24 hours of release with 100+ Million views. DYNAMITE debuted at number one on the US BillBoard Hot 100 chart making it the first Asian Act to do so.
